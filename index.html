Get Started Here


If you have recently received your Lowe’s credit card, the first step is to activate it online. You can easily do this by visiting lowes.syf.com/activate. This secure activation portal allows you to verify your details and start using your card within minutes.


Where do I activate my Lowe’s card?

To activate your card using lowes.syf.com/activate, follow these steps:


  1. Visit the official activation page: Go to lowes.syf.com/activate using your browser. Enter your card details and required information to begin.

  2. Verify your identity: Provide the necessary personal details such as ZIP code or last digits of your Social Security Number to confirm your identity and complete activation.

How do I activate Lowe’s credit card online?

Activating your Lowe’s credit card online is simple and managed by Synchrony Bank. Follow these steps carefully:


  • Open a secure web browser on your phone, tablet, or computer to avoid security risks.
  • Go to the official activation website: lowes.syf.com/activate, the secure portal provided by Synchrony Bank.
  • Enter your Lowe's credit card number exactly as it appears on your card.
  • Provide your full name as printed on the card.
  • Enter the security code (CVV) from the back of your card for verification.
  • Input additional required details, such as the last four digits of your Social Security Number or billing ZIP code.
  • Review all entered information for accuracy to prevent delays.
  • Click the Activate or Submit button to send your details securely to Synchrony Bank.
  • Wait for the confirmation message indicating your card has been successfully activated.
  • Once activated, log in to your online account to check balances, make payments, and manage your Lowe's credit card easily.
